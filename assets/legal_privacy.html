Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 12, 2026

SeekAPI Global (HK) Ltd complies with the Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance (Cap. 486) of Hong Kong.

1. Zero-Retention Policy

SeekAPI.ai implements a Zero-Retention Policy for API payloads. We do NOT store, log, or cache the content of your prompts or the resulting AI completions. Data is processed in volatile memory and streamed directly. We do not use customer data to train or fine-tune AI models.

2. Data We Collect

We collect minimal metadata necessary for service delivery: (a) Account email for authentication; (b) Billing metadata for Stripe processing; (c) IP addresses for DDoS mitigation and rate-limiting. This metadata is encrypted and stored on secure servers with restricted access.

3. Third-Party Processors

Payment processing is handled exclusively by Stripe, Inc.. SeekAPI does not store credit card numbers or CVV codes. Your financial data is governed by Stripe's Global Privacy Policy.

4. International Data Transfers

By using our Service, you acknowledge that data may be routed through international data centers to optimize latency and compute costs. All transfers are conducted using industry-standard encryption (TLS 1.3).
