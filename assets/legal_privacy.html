Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Compliance with PDPO

We comply with the Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance (Cap. 486) of Hong Kong. We collect minimal data: (i) Email for account verification; (ii) Metadata for billing; (iii) Temporary logs for security debugging.

2. No Data Retention Policy

SeekAPI.ai does not store the content of your API requests or responses. Data is streamed directly to/from upstream providers.

3. Financial Data & Stripe

All payment processing is handled by Stripe, Inc. We do not store credit card numbers on our infrastructure.
