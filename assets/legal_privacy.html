Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 13, 2026

SeekAPI Global (HK) Ltd complies with the Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance (Cap. 486) of Hong Kong.

1. ZERO-RETENTION PAYLOAD POLICY

SeekAPI.ai implements a strict Zero-Retention Policy for API request and response payloads. We do NOT store, log, or cache the content of your prompts or the resulting AI completions. Data is processed in volatile memory and streamed directly to upstream providers. We do not use customer data to train or fine-tune AI models.

2. METADATA COLLECTION

We collect minimal metadata necessary for service delivery: (a) Account email for authentication; (b) Billing metadata for Stripe processing; (c) Technical logs (IP addresses, timestamps) for DDoS mitigation and rate-limiting. These logs are encrypted and automatically purged every 14 days.

3. THIRD-PARTY PROCESSORS (STRIPE)

Payment processing is handled exclusively by Stripe, Inc.. SeekAPI does not store credit card numbers, CVVs, or full billing addresses on our infrastructure. Your financial data is subject to Stripe's Global Privacy Policy.

4. INTERNATIONAL DATA TRANSFERS

By using our Service, you acknowledge that data may be routed through international data centers to optimize latency. All transfers are conducted using industry-standard TLS 1.3 encryption.
