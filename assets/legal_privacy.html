Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 13, 2026

We comply with the Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance (Cap. 486) of Hong Kong.

1. Zero-Retention Policy

SeekAPI.ai does NOT store, log, or inspect the content of your API requests. Data is processed in volatile memory and streamed directly to upstream providers.

2. Data Collection

We only collect: (a) Email for account management; (b) Billing metadata; (c) IP addresses for security. This data is encrypted and never shared.

3. Payment Processing

All transactions are handled by Stripe, Inc.. We do not store credit card details.
