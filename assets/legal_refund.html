Refund & Billing Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. 7-Day Refund Window

We offer a full refund for unused, unconsumed credits within 7 days of the original transaction. Once a refund is processed, the associated credits will be voided.

2. Non-Refundable Consumptions

Tokens consumed for successful API calls are strictly non-refundable. In the event of a technical failure, credits will be re-credited.

3. Anti-Fraud Measures

We reserve the right to freeze accounts suspected of fraud. Unauthorized chargebacks may result in legal action in the HKSAR.
