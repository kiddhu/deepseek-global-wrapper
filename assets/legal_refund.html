Refund & Billing Policy

Last Updated: March 12, 2026

1. Pre-paid Credit System

SeekAPI.ai operates on a pre-paid credit model. Credits are deducted in real-time based on token consumption as reported by upstream providers. 1 USD is equivalent to 500,000 standard credits, subject to model-specific multipliers.

2. 7-Day Refund Guarantee

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for the UNUSED and UNCONSUMED balance of your initial purchase. If you are unsatisfied with the service quality, you may request a refund by contacting support@seekapi.ai within 7 days of the transaction date.

3. Non-Refundable Items

Credits that have already been consumed for successful API calls (HTTP 200) are strictly non-refundable. Promotional credits, bonus balances, and trial credits have no cash value and cannot be refunded.

4. Chargeback & Fraud Prevention

Unauthorized chargebacks will result in immediate account termination and permanent blacklisting of the associated domain and IP range. SeekAPI reserves the right to pursue legal action in the HKSAR to recover funds lost through fraudulent chargebacks.
