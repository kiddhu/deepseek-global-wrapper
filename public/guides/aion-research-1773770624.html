AION RESEARCH INSIGHTS

Neural document expansion for ad-hoc information retrieval

Original Abstract: Recently, Nogueira et al. [2019] proposed a new approach to document expansion based on a neural Seq2Seq model, showing significant improvement on short text retrieval task. However, this approach needs a large amount of in-domain training data. In this paper, we show that this neural document expansion approach can be effectively adapted to standard IR tasks, where labels are scarce and many long documents are present.

核心突破

这篇论文的核心突破在于，它成功地将Nogueira等人提出的、依赖大量领域内数据的神经序列到序列（Seq2Seq）文档扩展模型，适配到了标签稀缺且包含大量长文档的标准信息检索（IR）任务中。它证明了通过巧妙的模型调整与训练策略，可以克服数据瓶颈，将前沿的神经扩展技术从狭窄的短文本任务推广到更通用、更现实的检索场景。这是一次从“实验室理想条件”到“工业界复杂环境”的有效迁移。
